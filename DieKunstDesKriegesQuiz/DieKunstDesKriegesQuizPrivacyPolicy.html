Die Kunst des Krieges Privacy Policy

The app wants to access for your identity, your location and your media files on the phone. Actually this permissions are not necessary to provide functionality but a third party library added this requirements to the manifest file. The permissions of an app are defined in its manifest. When I include the third party library (which is for the particles), the requirements of the library are merged with my manifest and I cannot delete them. This app does not use your location, your files or your identity. The permissions are required without beeing used because of the library. The network permissions are necessary to display ads on your phone.
